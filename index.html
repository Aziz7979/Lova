Our Services

Immigration Law

Our team specialises in a wide range of UK Visas, nationality and asylum applications to the UK Border Agency and the Home Office. Our expert immigration lawyers can provide advice, practical assistance and professional representation to guide you through the application and decision-making process from start to finish. We were directly involved in a landmark ruling at the Royal Courts of Justice in 2013, a precedent-setting decision which prevented the deportation of Tamil asylum seekers.

Conveyancing

Whether you are buying a new office or purchasing land or doing anything in relation to a property or land, to buying or selling your home, there are considerable obligations and responsibilities. This usually involves one of the largest financial transactions you are likely to undertake. As such it is crucial that you instruct experienced solicitors who know how to handle the conveyancing and any other legal matters involved in what is a very complicated process.
