No-one deserves to be put at risk. If you have had an accident that was not your fault you could be entitled to receive financial compensation.

Lova Solicitors can provide expert legal advice, and help to secure a payment to cover the financial damages that you suffered as a result of the injury. We understand that no-one expects to have an accident but we believe that professional, legal help should be available to all. This is why we offer our services on a no-win, no-fee basis, removing the risk from making a claim.

Personal injury claims can result from a range of situations where another person or organisation was at fault. This might be an accident on the road or caused by professional carelessness. Whatever your circumstances, it is not fair that you should suffer the consequences for another person’s actions. We will help you to receive the maximum financial compensation you are entitled to, in the shortest possible time.

Claims for personal injury can become protracted and time-consuming but we have many years of experience in this field. We have access to some of the best medical experts to help expedite your case by providing evidence that is irrefutable.

Financial compensation is sometimes also referred to as “damages” and is divided into two specific areas: general and special.

For serious injury solicitors, we are the leading team of experts to go to. We provide services for many other types of personal injuries such as:

  • -Spinal injury
  • -Head injury
  • -Back injury
  • -Accident injury
  • -Car accident
  • -Road traffic accident

General damages are designed to cover “pain, suffering, and loss of amenity”. This means the amount you are awarded depends on the severity and extent of your injuries, and the likely prognosis.

It is important to ensure that you are fully compensated for the losses you experience as a result of your injury. In cases which are severe, there may be no financial payment which adequately covers your loss, but Farani Taylor will work hard to get the maximum compensation available under the law. We have extensive knowledge and experience in personal injury and will assess your case quickly and professionally. As we are the Top Personal Injury Law Firm.

We believe in keeping things simple so we always work on a no-win, no-fee basis and pay out the money once received, not before.
