Direction to our office

Our office is very close to the East Ham Tube station. You can reach east ham by using either District or Hammersmith and City Line.

As you come out of the East Ham Tube Station turn right to the high street and follow the road and cross the traffic signal, walk about 6 to 8 minutes then you will see our office on the right-hand side of the high street north.

The Lova Solicitors is the best solicitors firm in and around East London area. Our clients are 100% satisfied with our services. We are based in East Ham, our clients are from all sort of multi cultural background.
